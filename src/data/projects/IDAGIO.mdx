## 01 Overview

-- This senior art direction + interaction design project imagines an expressive post-event microsite for the _what if?_ exhibition. Hosted by [fabrica](https://www.fabrica.it/), the event explores the infinite possibilities of our future, featuring works from 12 international artists who are at the end of their art residency.
