## 01 Overview

-- Bump is a project created for an intensive 24 hours prototype [competition](https://www.protothon.info/) hosted by [DubsTech](https://dubstech.org/) (University of Washington). It was awarded the 1st place amongst 38 competiting solutions by a panel of industry professionals from Google, Facebook, Salesforce etc.
