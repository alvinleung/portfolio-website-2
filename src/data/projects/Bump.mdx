## 01 Overview

-- Bump is a project created for an intensive 24 hours UI prototyping [competition](https://www.protothon.info/) hosted by [DubsTech](https://dubstech.org/) (University of Washington). The project was awarded the 1<sup>st</sup> place amongst 38 competiting solutions by a panel of industry professionals from Google, Facebook, Salesforce etc.
